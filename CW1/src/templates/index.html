This website provides a list of Cage the Elephant music videos and singles.

Cage the Elephant is an American rock band from Kentucky, USA. The band consists of vocalist Matthew Shultz, rhythm guitarist Brad Shultz, drummer Jared Champion and bassist Daniel Tichenor, as well as touring keyboardist/guitarist Matthan Minster and touring lead guitarist Nick Bockrath. The band is known all over the United States and the United Kingdom since they released their first album 'Cage the Elephant' in 2008.
